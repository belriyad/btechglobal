About B Tech Global LLC

Your Partner in AI and Technology Innovation

Our Story

B Tech Global LLC is a premier AI and technology consulting firm based in Doha, Qatar. We were founded with a clear mission: to democratize access to cutting-edge technology and AI solutions for organizations of all sizes.

We understand that not everyone speaks the language of technology, and that's where we excel. Our team specializes in bridging the gap between complex technical concepts and practical business applications, making innovation accessible to non-technical founders, educators, and organizational leaders.

Our Mission

To empower startups, organizations, and educational institutions with the knowledge, tools, and strategies needed to harness AI and technology for transformative impact. We believe every great idea deserves expert execution.

Our Vision

To be the leading technology consulting partner in the Middle East, known for turning ambitious ideas into successful realities through strategic guidance, AI innovation, and structured execution.
