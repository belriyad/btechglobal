Frequently Asked Questions

What industries do you work with?

We work with organizations across various industries including startups, technology companies, educational institutions, government entities, healthcare, finance, and more. Our expertise in AI and technology consulting is applicable across sectors.

Do you work with clients outside Qatar?

Yes! While we're based in Doha, Qatar, we serve clients across the Middle East and internationally. We offer both in-person and virtual consulting services to accommodate different locations and preferences.

What is your typical engagement timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Short-term engagements can be as brief as a few weeks for focused advisory work, while comprehensive implementations may span several months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

How do your pricing and payment work?

We offer flexible pricing models including project-based fees, retainer arrangements, and hourly consulting rates. Pricing depends on the scope, duration, and complexity of the engagement. Contact us for a customized quote.

Can you help if we have limited technical knowledge?

Absolutely! In fact, this is one of our specialties. We excel at working with non-technical founders and leaders, explaining complex concepts in clear terms, and guiding you through technology decisions with confidence.

What makes your approach different?

We combine deep technical expertise with a practical, hands-on approach. Unlike consultants who only deliver reports, we partner with you through implementation, ensuring strategies become reality. We also focus on knowledge transfer to build your internal capabilities.
